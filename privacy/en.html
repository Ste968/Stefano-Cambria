Photo Manager Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

Photo Manager Pro is a 100% offline macOS application. It processes your photos locally on your Mac and never uploads any data to the internet.

Data That Stays Local

  • Photo content
  • Photo metadata (EXIF, GPS)
  • File names and folder structure
  • GPS coordinates

GeoNames Database

The app uses GeoNames.org database (CC BY 4.0 license) for geolocation. This data is downloaded and stored only in your Mac's sandbox container.

No Analytics, No Tracking

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. No analytics, no telemetry, no account required.
